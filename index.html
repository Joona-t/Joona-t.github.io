─ □ ✕
Mac & iOS App · Free

Learn every Japanese kana — free, forever. Hiragana and katakana taught the way the loved-but-pricey apps do: vivid mnemonics, real SM-2 spaced repetition, recognition → recall → stroke-order writing, and streaks. Cozy Sleep 🌙
─ □ ✕
Sparky Experiment

Not another sleep tracker — Apple Health and Oura already do that. Cozy Sleep is a reasoning layer on top: it pulls in your sleep data plus meds, caffeine, training, and stress, and stitches one honest plain-English read on why you slept the way you did.

Testing this out as a personal recovery-intelligence experiment. Likely lands inside Sparky / the LoveSpark ecosystem rather than shipping as a standalone app.

1 ▸

Everything here is something I needed and built. Each tool is a knowledge gap I'm filling out loud — small experiments in the calmer, kinder internet I want to use. If they're useful to you too, that's a quiet bonus.

I'm experimenting with a handful of apps and tools, and I picture them all converging on Sparky — a local brain for AI agents and any LLM provider you choose. Local only; nothing leaves your machine. I'm using, learning from, and testing each one as I build it out.

Right now my focus is deeper work that isn't listed here yet — I'm choosing depth and learning. Think of this as a living snapshot, not a finished shelf.

Full source and the unrestricted builds live on my GitHub if you want to install, modify, or learn along with me.
